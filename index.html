About MyVoiceGSA

We are Carleton University graduate students from diverse professional and educational backgrounds, representing different countries around the world. As a group of proactive leaders, we aim to support all graduate students and have decided to participate in the GSA election 2025.

With our slogan "MyVoiceGSA," we're running in this election to leverage our expertise and experiences to make a meaningful difference. Together, we aim to create a platform that provides resources and support to all graduate students through various positions in the GSA community.

GSA Election 2025

Important Dates

  • Nomination Period: March 19 - March 30, 2025
  • Campaign Period: March 31 - April 7, 2025
  • All Candidates Forum: April 7, 2025
  • Voting Period: April 9-10, 2025

Election Platform

Online Voting System

The GSA elections are hosted online using the OIRP voting software administered by Carleton University. Elections results will be tabulated through the software and share to the Elections Officer who will then announce them.

Key Features:
  • Secure and confidential voting process
  • Easy-to-use interface
  • Automated result tabulation
